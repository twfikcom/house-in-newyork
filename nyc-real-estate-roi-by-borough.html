Investing in New York Real Estate: Which Borough Offers the Best ROI?

Multi-family homes in the Bronx and Queens currently offer the highest cap rates for investors looking for long-term rental income.

Multi-family homes in The Bronx and Queens are currently showing some of the city’s highest cap rates. As Manhattan becomes saturated, savvy investors are looking toward these growth corridors.

While Manhattan offers long-term appreciation and stability, the yield-hungry investor is looking toward the "Next Brooklyn" – potentially certain pockets of the Rockaways or Northern Staten Island.
