The Pros and Cons of Buying a Townhouse vs. a Condo in New York

Do you want full ownership of the land (Townhouse) or the convenience of a serviced building (Condo)? We compare rights and maintenance costs.

Do you want the autonomy of a townhouse or the amenities of a condo? The choice defines your New York lifestyle and your future resale value.

Townhouses offer fee-simple ownership and no monthly board fees, but you are responsible for everything from snow removal to roof repairs. Condos provide convenience and high-end services but come with common charges.
