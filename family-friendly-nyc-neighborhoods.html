HOUSE IN NEW YORK

The Best Family-Friendly Neighborhoods in New York for Homeowners

School districts are the primary driver for property value in Brooklyn and Queens. Neighborhoods like Park Slope and Bayside lead the charts.

For families, the home is only as good as the school district it sits in. Park Slope and Brooklyn Heights continue to lead the pack due to their historic brownstones and proximity to Prospect Park.

However, newer hotspots in Long Island City and Astoria are offering incredible amenities for young families, including newly constructed schools and massive waterfront parks.
